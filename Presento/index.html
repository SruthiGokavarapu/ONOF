About One Nation- One Funding

To enhance its quality of life, our billion-strong nation deserves nurturing and care in the form of basic education. It has a prominent and disciplinary role in balancing the country's socioeconomic structure. This calls for the all-round development of our citizens, which can be achieved by building strong foundations in education. In pursuance of this goal, One Funding-One Nation featuring all the available funding schemes provided by the funding agencies across the country to various higher educational institutions in the streams of Science and Technology, Literacy, Minority Upliftment, Skill Development, Industrial Research and Sports.

The Objective of One Nation- One Funding

  • It aids in the development of higher educational institutions with modern infrastructural facilities in laboratory (s), workshop (s), and computing facilities in order to enhance functional efficiency for teaching, training, and research purposes
  • Planned development, includes increasing access to education and enhancing the quality of educational institutions across the country, even in communities where people do not have easy access to it.

Steps for registration as a funding agency:

  • Go to the top of the page and click on Sign-Up.
  • The page redirects to Apply as a HEI's. Click on the FUND ORG at right side and Apply as a Hirer will be displayed.
  • Fill the required mandatory details.
  • The required certificates are Organisation Certificate, GST Certificate and click on the Register.

Steps for registration as a HEI's:

  • Go to the top of the page and click on Sign-Up.
  • The page redirects to Apply as a HEI's and will be displayed. Fill the required mandatory details.
  • The required certificates are Address proof, Income proof and proof of identity of institution and click on the Register.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is One Funding-One Nation?

    One Funding-One Nation features all the available funding schemes provided by the funding agencies across the country to various higher educational institutions in the streams of Science and Technology, Literacy, Minority Upliftment, Skill Development, Industrial Research and Sports

  • How does it help to the institutions?

    It helps to upgrade the higher education system by giving funding under various projects/schemes.

  • When will I receive funds that raised?

    As soon as your fundraiser has raised a certain amount, you can request for withdrawal. Once we have verified your bank details, funds will be transferred within 5-7 working days. You can request multiple fund withdrawals whenever needed.

  • What do I need to start a fundraiser?

    You can start a fundraiser immediately and add relevant images and information to tell donors why you require funds. One Funding-One Nation will then reach out to verify your information through identity proofs and other relevant documents. Begin creating your fundraiser and get stepwise help from our experts to run it!

  • How does it cost to raise funds on One Funding-One Nation?

    You can start a fundraiser for free on One Funding-One Nation. Depending on your fundraising needs, One Funding-One Nation a certain percentage is deducted from the amount you raised as fees. Recently, One Funding-One Nation introduced fundraising at 0% platform fees to help you get the maximum of the amount you raise.

  • Do we need to return the funds that raised?

    Money contributed towards your fundraiser on One Funding-One Nation is considered a donation or a gift and you are not liable to return the money back at any point. This no payback liability ensures you can pay for development of institution need without the worry of debt.
